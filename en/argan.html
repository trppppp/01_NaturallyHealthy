Argan Oil

Oil obtained from cold-pressed roasted argan almonds, using traditional methods.

Nutritional properties

Argan oil is characterized by its excellent nutritional properties thanks to its rich content of vitamin E, carotenes and unsaturated fatty acids that contribute to the proper functioning of the cardiovascular system. Helps prevent obesity.

Culinary uses

Its exotic aroma and fruity flavor makes it highly recommended for dressing salads, soups, meat and fish.
