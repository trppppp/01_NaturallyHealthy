Avocado oil

Oil obtained from cold-pressed avocado pulp using traditional methods.

Nutritional properties

Its rich content of oleic acid and monounsaturated fatty acids protects the cardiovascular system. It contains antioxidants, minerals, beta-carotene and vitamins B, E and K that strengthen the body in a general way.

Culinary uses

Its fruity and rich flavor makes it the perfect dressing for vegetables, meat and fish. It serves as a base for sauces and condiments.
